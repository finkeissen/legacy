# Scalability ≠ Generality

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Scalable systems perform under increased load.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Scalability is treated as universal applicability.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From performance expansion to domain generality.

## Structural Residue
Generality requires conceptual adaptability.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Growth suggests universality.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
