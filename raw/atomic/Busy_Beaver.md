# Busy Beaver Function

## Core Result
The Busy Beaver function grows faster than any computable function.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Its growth is taken as evidence of absolute cognitive or metaphysical limits.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Non-computability is inflated into unknowability.

## Philosophical Residue
The function demonstrates limits of computation, not of understanding.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Extreme growth suggests transcendence.
