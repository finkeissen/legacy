# Forcing

## Core Result
Forcing constructs model extensions in which certain statements hold or fail.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Truth is taken to be manufactured or relative.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Model extension is confused with creation of facts.

## Philosophical Residue
Forcing manipulates descriptions, not ontology.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
The language of “adding” sets invites reification.
