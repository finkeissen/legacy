# Non-measurable Sets

## Core Result
Assuming the axiom of choice, there exist sets that cannot be assigned a Lebesgue measure.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
This is taken to show mathematics is detached from reality.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Formal existence is confused with physical or empirical significance.

## Philosophical Residue
The result concerns abstract measure theory, not applicability.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Measure is learned empirically, making abstraction feel paradoxical.
