# Hierarchy ≠ Priority

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Hierarchical ordering structures components but does not establish importance or fundamentality.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Higher levels are treated as more real or more important.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From structural position to ontological or normative priority.

## Structural Residue
Hierarchy is an organizational tool, not a value claim.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Vertical orderings suggest depth or fundamentality.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
