# Bell’s Theorem

## Core Result
Bell’s theorem shows that no local hidden-variable theory can reproduce all quantum predictions.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
It is read as proving metaphysical non-locality or broken causality.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Constraints on theoretical models are taken as direct ontological claims.

## Philosophical Residue
The theorem restricts classes of explanations, not reality itself.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Experimental violation of inequalities suggests dramatic metaphysical conclusions.
