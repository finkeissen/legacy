# Kolmogorov Complexity

## Core Result
Kolmogorov complexity measures the length of the shortest description of an object.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Randomness is treated as an intrinsic property of objects.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Description-relative measures are mistaken for ontological attributes.

## Philosophical Residue
Complexity depends on representation, not essence.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Compression intuitions mimic metaphysical simplicity.
