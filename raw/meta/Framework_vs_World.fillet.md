# Framework ≠ World

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Frameworks structure interpretation and analysis.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Framework constraints are treated as world constraints.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From framework necessity to necessity of reality.

## Structural Residue
Frameworks delimit description, not existence.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Frameworks feel invisible once adopted.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
