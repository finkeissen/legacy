# Explanation implies justification

**Error Group:** Category Errors

## Description
This document records a recurring conceptual error observed across individuals,
institutions, and large-scale coordination systems.

The error consists in assuming:

> Explanation implies justification

## Why This Is a Problem
This assumption collapses boundaries, categories, or system dynamics that must
remain explicit in formally bounded analysis.

## Typical Consequences
- Systematic misinterpretation of signals
- Invalid transfers of authority or scope
- Structural fragility in decision-making systems
