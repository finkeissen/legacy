# Case Description

## Overview
The archived material is carefully written, cautious in tone,
and largely accurate. It would normally qualify for reuse.

## Context
The material was produced as guidance with explicit caveats,
and has been cited positively in several downstream contexts.

## Inadmissibility rationale
Despite surface rigor, the core justification relies on a
single unverified premise that cannot be independently validated.

Removing or weakening that premise collapses the argument.
Because the premise cannot be rehabilitated without external
assumptions, the material is classified as STOP.

## Boundary
This case is intentionally close to admissible.
It exists to test whether the system can enforce STOP
without escalating categories or relaxing standards.
