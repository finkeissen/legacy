# Stability ≠ Optimality

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Stable systems persist over time.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Persistence is treated as optimality or superiority.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From temporal survival to normative evaluation.

## Structural Residue
Stability only indicates resistance to change.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
What lasts feels right.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
