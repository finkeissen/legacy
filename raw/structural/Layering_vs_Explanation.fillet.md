# Layering ≠ Explanation

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Layered models organize complexity across levels.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Layer separation is treated as explanatory depth.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From architectural layering to causal or explanatory understanding.

## Structural Residue
Layers describe interfaces, not causes.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Depth metaphors suggest understanding.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
