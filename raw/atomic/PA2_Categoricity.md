# Categoricity of Second-Order Arithmetic

## Core Result
Second-order Peano arithmetic is categorical under full second-order semantics.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
This is taken to establish a uniquely real structure of arithmetic.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Semantic strength is confused with ontological privilege.

## Philosophical Residue
Categoricity depends on semantic assumptions.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Uniqueness invites metaphysical reification.
