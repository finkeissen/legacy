# Church–Turing Thesis

## Core Claim
The Church–Turing thesis identifies effective calculability with Turing computability. It is not a theorem, but a methodological thesis.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
It is often treated as a necessary truth about minds, nature, or physical reality.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
A pragmatic identification is elevated into an ontological law.

## Philosophical Residue
The thesis frames computation but does not legislate metaphysics.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Its extraordinary empirical success invites reification into a universal constraint.
