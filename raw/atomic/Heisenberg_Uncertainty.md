# Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle

## Core Result
The uncertainty principle sets lower bounds on simultaneous operator variances in quantum mechanics.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
It is taken to show that reality itself is indeterminate.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Formal constraints on measurement are inflated into metaphysical indeterminism.

## Philosophical Residue
The principle concerns theoretical structure, not ontology.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Measurement limits are mistaken for limits of being.
