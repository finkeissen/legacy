# Reflection Principles

## Core Result
Reflection principles allow certain meta-theoretic facts to be internalized incrementally.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
They are taken to show that the mind necessarily transcends formal systems.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Meta-level reasoning is converted into metaphysical dualism.

## Philosophical Residue
Reflection concerns formal strength, not cognition.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Informal reasoning is mistaken for non-formal capacity.
