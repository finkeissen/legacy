# Modularity ≠ Independence

## Level
Structural (E2)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Modules can be separated for design without being independent in reality.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Modular design is treated as ontological separation.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From engineering convenience to claims of independence.

## Structural Residue
Modularity is pragmatic, not metaphysical.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Clean boundaries suggest separability.

## Useful Checks
- **Ordering vs. explanation:** Does the structure explain anything, or merely organize?
- **Counterfactual:** Would the claim survive a re-partitioning or re-layering?
- **Scope:** Is authority inferred from coherence alone?
