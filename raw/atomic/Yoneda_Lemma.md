# Yoneda Lemma

## Core Result
The Yoneda lemma characterizes objects by their relations to all other objects in a category.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Relational characterizations are read as ontological definitions.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Structural representation is mistaken for metaphysical identity.

## Philosophical Residue
Yoneda concerns representability, not being.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Explanatory power is conflated with ontological priority.
