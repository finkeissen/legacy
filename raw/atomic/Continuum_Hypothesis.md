# Continuum Hypothesis

## Core Result
The continuum hypothesis is independent of standard ZFC set theory.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Independence is taken to show that mathematical reality is indeterminate.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Model-theoretic independence is mistaken for ontological indeterminacy.

## Philosophical Residue
The result concerns axiom choice, not reality itself.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Undecidability is conflated with metaphysical openness.
