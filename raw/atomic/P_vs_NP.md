# P vs NP Problem

## Core Question
The P vs NP problem asks whether every efficiently verifiable problem is also efficiently solvable.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
It is often claimed that a separation would prove human creativity non-algorithmic.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
Computational complexity distinctions are read as cognitive or metaphysical claims.

## Philosophical Residue
The problem concerns resource bounds, not the nature of intelligence.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Efficiency gaps invite anthropomorphic interpretation of difficulty.
