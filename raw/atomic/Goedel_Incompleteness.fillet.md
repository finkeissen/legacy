# Gödel – Incompleteness as Metaphysics (Fallacy)

## Core Claim / Pattern
Gödel shows limits of formal systems regarding provability.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
This is taken as proof that truth transcends logic or that mind surpasses machines.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From formal proof limits to metaphysical claims about truth or mind.

## Philosophical Residue
Results concern formal systems; metaphysical extensions require extra premises.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
‘True but unprovable’ sounds metaphysically deep.

## Useful Checks
- **Bridge principle?** What additional assumption connects the formal/epistemic result to a claim about reality?
- **Scope?** Does the statement concern *models/concepts/methods* or *being itself*?
- **Counterfactual:** What would have to change for the result to stop holding — the world or the framework?
