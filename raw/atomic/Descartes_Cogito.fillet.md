# Descartes – Cogito and Certainty

## Core Claim / Pattern
The Cogito establishes minimal certainty within methodical doubt: doubting presupposes thinking.

## Frequent Overinterpretation
Methodological certainty becomes a metaphysical proof of a substantial self.

## Where the Interpretive Transfer Occurs
From epistemic indubitability to ontological substance claims.

## Philosophical Residue
The Cogito secures a starting point for knowledge; further ontology requires additional arguments.

## Why the Misinterpretation Is Tempting
Certainty feels like existence-proof.

## Useful Checks
- **Bridge principle?** What additional assumption connects the formal/epistemic result to a claim about reality?
- **Scope?** Does the statement concern *models/concepts/methods* or *being itself*?
- **Counterfactual:** What would have to change for the result to stop holding — the world or the framework?
